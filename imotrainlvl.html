International Maritime Organization (IMO) Training

Our IMO Level Training is designed to prepare your team with the expertise needed to handle oil spill incidents in compliance with the International Maritime Organization (IMO) standards. From operational response to management, our comprehensive training programs are tailored to different levels of personnel across various industries.

  • Certified Instructors: Led by internationally recognized experts in oil spill response.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Covering topics such as oil spill prevention, response, and cleanup techniques.
  • Hands-on Drills: Practical simulations that prepare your team for real-life scenarios.
  • Flexible Learning: Customizable modules that can be delivered on-site or remotely.

Our IMO Training Levels

IMO Level 1

Introduction to oil spill response, designed for personnel responsible for on-site operations during oil spill incidents.

IMO Level 2

For supervisors and managers who are responsible for coordinating oil spill response strategies and resources.

IMO Level 3

Advanced training for decision-makers and senior managers overseeing large-scale oil spill response operations.
