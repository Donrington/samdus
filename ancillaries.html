Ancillaries

Explore our range of essential ancillary equipment designed for efficient and effective oil spill response operations.

OPA90 Deck Spill Kit

The OPA90 Deck Spill Kit is a versatile and comprehensive solution, meticulously designed in collaboration with leading ship owners and operators. This kit includes essential components such as oil absorbents, hand tools, containers, and pumps, ensuring rapid response to spill incidents on deck. Compact and cost-effective, this kit can be strategically positioned in multiple locations, enhancing preparedness for any spill scenario. The kit's robust, waterproof, and impact-resistant storage container ensures durability and reliability in harsh maritime environments. Additionally, the kit's contents can be customized to meet specific customer requirements, providing flexibility and adaptability in spill response planning.

OPA90 Deck Spill Kit - Essential for rapid response to spill incidents on deck.

Boulder Cleaner - Efficient and innovative solution for challenging oil spill scenarios.

Boulder Cleaner

The Boulder Cleaner represents a breakthrough in oil spill response technology, offering an innovative approach to cleaning oil spills in challenging conditions. This hydraulically driven unit is compatible with any self-priming suction pump or RO-Vac unit, allowing operators to recover oil from difficult surfaces such as rocks with ease. The Boulder Cleaner features a lightweight, seawater-resistant aluminum construction, ensuring durability and ease of handling. The skimmer head, equipped with a small hydraulic motor, efficiently powers the brush unit to remove oil while the operator safely directs the suction lance to hard-to-reach areas.

Containers

Our containers are available in both economical standard and custom versions, tailored for shipping, storage, and transportation of equipment and materials. Ranging from 8ft to 40ft, these containers are constructed from high-quality materials and can be customized with ISO corners, custom doors, and forklift channels as needed. DESMI's containers have a long-standing reputation for durability and reliability, having been supplied to customers worldwide for over 30 years. Many of these containers are still in use today, demonstrating their longevity and value.

Ready for Dispatch Systems - Complete, ready-to-deploy equipment packages for rapid response.

Ready for Dispatch (RFD) Systems

Our Ready for Dispatch (RFD) systems are designed to provide complete, ready-to-deploy equipment packages for various spill response scenarios, from offshore to shoreline operations. These systems include booms, skimmers, power packs, and hoses, all packaged in DNV-certified containers and ready for immediate dispatch. Our attention to detail ensures that every component, from spark arrestors to hydraulic hoses, is included, enabling efficient and effective deployment in emergency situations.

Wide Spray Dispersant Systems

The Wide Spray Dispersant System is a versatile solution for effective oil dispersion, featuring a diesel-driven pump set, adjustable spray arms with specialized nozzles, and a comprehensive hose system for seawater, dispersant, and discharge. This system can be customized to fit various vessel sizes and is easily transferable between vessels, making it a highly flexible tool for oil spill response operations.

Wide Spray Dispersant Systems - Flexible and efficient solutions for oil dispersion.

Slickgone NS Type 3 Dispersant - Effective and low-toxicity solution for various oil types.

Dispersant - Slickgone NS Type 3

Our Slickgone NS Type 3 Dispersant is a highly effective and environmentally friendly solution, approved by numerous international organizations for use on a wide range of oil types, including those with high wax content. This low-toxicity dispersant can be combined with our Wide Spray Dispersant System to create a comprehensive shipboard dispersant solution, ensuring optimal oil dispersion and minimal environmental impact.
