Tanks

Durable and Reliable Storage Solutions for Industrial Needs

RO-TANK

The RO-TANK is a high-quality, durable storage tank available in sizes from 5 to 50m3. It is designed for versatile use on land or floating on water (with the addition of an air pillow). The RO-TANK is constructed from robust, reinforced neoprene rubber with a Hypalon outer layer. The Hypalon skin offers high abrasion and puncture resistance, ensuring durability even with prolonged exposure to sunlight and various liquids. The tank lies flat when empty, allowing for compact storage and easy transport, making it ideal for a wide range of operations.

RO-TANK - Versatile storage solution for Oil applications.

RO-TANK Cleaning System - Efficient cleaning solutions for maintaining tank integrity.

RO-TANK Cleaning

Introducing a new cleaning system for the RO-TANK, designed for efficient and thorough cleaning without removing the ends. The innovative spray probe can be inserted through a porthole in the tank after partial air inflation and operated with a pressure pump, such as those in the DOP series. This system effectively removes stubborn pollutants and cleans hard-to-reach areas within the tank. The cleaning process is environmentally friendly, preventing any leakage, and drainage can be completed during or after operations through existing connections. This upgradeable system offers a cost-effective solution for maintaining tank cleanliness and integrity.

TROILTANK

The TROILTANK is a rapid deployment storage solution available in sizes from 1,000 to 10,000 litres, designed for use on land. Supported by a lightweight, quickly erected frame, the TROILTANK is simple and quick to deploy. Manufactured from PU or PVC, these tanks are extremely light and easy to transport. They are suitable for the temporary storage of various liquids, including oils and wastewater, as well as oiled materials prior to disposal. The TROILTANK is an essential addition to any oil spill cleanup operation, providing a versatile and lightweight solution for temporary storage needs.

TROILTANK - Quick deployment storage solution for temporary liquid and material storage.
