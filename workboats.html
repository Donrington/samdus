Workboats

Explore our range of versatile and reliable workboats, designed for various maritime operations.

POLLCAT

The POLLCAT is a robust steel catamaran workboat equipped with a built-in oil mop system, making it ideal for rapid response and recovery during oil spill events. Featuring a clear rear deck, integrated oil storage, and a hydraulic crane, the POLLCAT is an extremely versatile vessel. Its twin engines allow for extended operation times, while additional skimmers such as the TERMINATOR can be used for enhanced recovery capabilities. The POLLCAT also boasts a large cruising range and substantial pumping capacity for recovered oil, ensuring it remains operational in polluted areas for extended periods.

POLLCAT Workboat - Designed for rapid response and versatile operations.

POLLCRAFT Workboat - A multipurpose vessel with excellent stability and flexibility.

POLLCRAFT

The POLLCRAFT is a stable monohull workboat available in 10 or 12-meter lengths, providing a multipurpose platform for various marine operations. With a large, clear rear deck area and a hydraulic knuckle crane as standard, it is ideal for cleanup duties in ports, terminals, or harbors, and can handle more challenging tasks if necessary. The vessel offers a recovered oil capacity of 10m3 and a pumping capacity of 30m3/hr, enhancing its versatility and operational scope.

POLLKITTEN

The POLLKITTEN is the smallest vessel in our range, with a shallow draft perfect for sheltered terminal and harbor applications. Despite its size, it is equipped with a built-in oil mop system for rapid response to oil pollution. The POLLKITTEN has an oil recovery rate of up to 20m3 per hour and a pumping rate of up to 30m3 per hour. It also includes an option for a mounted knuckle crane and built-in storage for recovered oil, making it highly efficient for various cleanup operations.

POLLKITTEN Workboat - Small but powerful, perfect for confined spaces and rapid response.

Aluminium Workboats

In addition to our larger vessels, we offer a comprehensive range of smaller aluminium workboats. These vessels are ideal for a wide variety of harbor, inland, and coastal operations. Our range includes the Punt, Tiller, Tempest, Coastal, and Intensity models, available in sizes from 4.3m to 12.8m. A multitude of hull types, propulsion systems, and customization options such as wheelhouses, tow posts, and seating arrangements are available, ensuring we meet any customer specification or requirement.
