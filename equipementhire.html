Our Equipment Categories

Oil Skimmers

Our oil skimmers offer efficient recovery of oil from water surfaces, available in various configurations including disc, mop, and weir types for different oil viscosities.

Containment Booms

Our containment booms are designed to limit the spread of oil spills, effective in various environmental conditions, from calm waters to turbulent seas.

Power Packs

Portable, compact, and powerful, our power packs provide the necessary energy for remote operations and oil spill recovery efforts.

Vacuum Systems

Our vacuum systems enable rapid recovery of spilled oil and liquids from both water and ground surfaces, reducing environmental impact efficiently.
