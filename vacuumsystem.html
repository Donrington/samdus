Vacuum Systems

Innovative Solutions for Efficient Oil Recovery

RO-VAC MINI: Portable Vacuum and Transfer System

The RO-VAC MINI is a compact and portable vacuum system specifically engineered for the efficient suction of oils and liquids from ground surfaces or water bodies. Unlike conventional pumps, the RO-VAC MINI utilizes a high-powered vacuum pump capable of extracting oil from the surface of water or land with unmatched precision. An optional storage hopper provides a convenient solution for temporary storage of recovered oils, making the RO-VAC MINI a versatile tool for rapid deployment in diverse spill scenarios.

RO-VAC MINI - Portable vacuum system ideal for quick oil recovery from ground and water surfaces.

RO-VAC MKII - Versatile and powerful tool for suction of liquids and oils.

RO-VAC MKII: Advanced Vacuum System for Versatile Operations

The RO-VAC MKII is a robust solution for the extraction of oils and liquids from water surfaces, ground, or beach environments. Designed for versatility and maneuverability, the system is mounted on removable wheels with forklift channels, ensuring it can be easily transported across different terrains at a cost significantly lower than traditional vacuum trucks. With a diesel-powered vacuum pump capable of generating up to 95% vacuum and handling airflow up to 6,800 liters per minute, the RO-VAC MKII is also equipped with a high-pressure water pump, which can be used for washing or as an offloading pump as needed.

RO-TANKER: Efficient Transport and Disposal Solution

The RO-TANKER is available in 2, 4, and 5 cubic meter capacities and is engineered to work seamlessly with the RO-VAC MKII or other compatible vacuum pumps. Designed to provide the most versatile system for the transport and disposal of recovered oils and liquids, the RO-TANKER features large diameter wheels for off-road towing and a spacious rear access hatch for easy loading and unloading. Optional road lighting and fully braked wheel systems expand the operational capabilities of the tanker, making it an invaluable asset for diverse recovery operations.

RO-TANKER - A robust solution for transport and disposal of recovered oils and liquids.

RO VAC 4000 - Combined vacuum, storage, and transportation system.

RO VAC 4000: Integrated Vacuum and Storage Solution

The RO VAC 4000 combines the capabilities of the RO-VAC MKII and RO-TANKER 4000 into a single, highly efficient unit. This integrated system is mounted on a twin axle trailer and offers unparalleled versatility with options for powerful vacuum suction, discharge, and storage of recovered oil or fluids. With a capacity of 4000 liters, the RO VAC 4000 can be equipped with various accessories such as handheld vacuum lances, pressure washers, rock cleaners, and even a Mini-Max skimmer, enhancing operational efficiency in diverse conditions. Additional road lighting kits ensure safe operation on public roads, further expanding its utility and operational range.
