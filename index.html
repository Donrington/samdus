Welcome To Samdus

SAMDUS is a private and wholly owned Nigerian oilfield Services Company. Although incorporated in 2012, the company was founded by Nigerians with an extensive track record, market-leading insight, and experience in the Nigerian oil & gas industry. We currently operate only in Nigeria and are dedicated to our partners and communities through our enduring commitment and sustainability program.

Samdus is a global leader in the oil and gas industry, dedicated to advancing energy solutions that are both sustainable and efficient. With decades of experience and a commitment to innovation, we are shaping the future of energy.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional value through cutting-edge technology, unparalleled expertise, and a focus on safety and environmental stewardship.
